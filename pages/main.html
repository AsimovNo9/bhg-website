Welcome to the Bend Hackers Guild

The Guild is a place for geek enthusiasts of all types to gather, exchange information, learn new things, and have fun. Topics trend toward tech, but no topic is off limits.

Meetups

We have a meetup on the second Wednesday of the month, usually a presentation. Sometimes we have social gatherings (game night, movie night, etc.) on other days.

The Guild also teaches classes. Check the Meetup page or calendar for more information, and contact us if you want to teach one.
